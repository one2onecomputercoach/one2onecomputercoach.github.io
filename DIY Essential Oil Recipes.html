DIY Essential Oil Recipes

These easy to make essential oil blends are must-haves in any household and they are the most requested by my family members. 

Aches & Pains

14 drops eucalyptus
14 drops sweet marjoram or basil
7 drops lavender
7 drops peppermint
7 drops rosemary
Directions: Add essential oils to a 10ml roller bottle and fill the rest of the bottle with a carrier oil. Shake well. Apply directly to area of pain and massage gently in. 

Cuts & Scrapes

4 drops myrrh
4 drops frankincense
3 drops lavender
Directions: Add essential oils to a 10ml roller bottle and fill the rest of the bottle with fractionated coconut oil. Shake well. Apply directly to cuts and scrapes.


Healthy Nails

5 drops tea tree
3 drops lemon
2 drops peppermint
Directions: Add essential oils to a 10ml roller bottle and fill the rest of the bottle with a carrier oil. Shake well. Apply onto and around nails, Allow to dry.
