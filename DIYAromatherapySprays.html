DIY Aromatherapy 
Room & Linen Sprays

Below are some easy recipes for making your own room and linen sprays with essential oils.  These recipes were created with the intention of soothing and restoring balance to the doshas, which are the three Ayurvedic mind-body types. You can also make your own scents with your favorite essential oil.

Directions:  Add 20-30 drops of essential oil (single or blend of your choice) to a 2 oz spray bottle. Add 2 tablespoons of witch hazel. Mix until oil is well incorporated. Fill the rest of the bottle with distilled water. Shake well.

Serenity

This soothing scent provides aromatherapy to help restore balance to all three dosha types, making it especially suitable for those with the rare tridoshic constitution. 

12 drops sandalwood
8 drops frankincense
2 drops vanilla or myrrh


Love Spell

This romantic scent provides aromatherapy to help restore balance to Vata and Pitta dosha types.


8 drops sandalwood
8 drops ylang ylang
8 drops patchouli


* Essential oils are not intended to be a substitute for medical treatment. Discontinue use if rash, redness, or irritation occurs.
